02 — Context & challenge

Why it matters now

Modern security increasingly depends on the ability to observe, understand the situation and act fast — and to do so with one's own technology, without relying on external suppliers.

01

Technological sovereignty

Critical capabilities should be built inside the country — from design to series production.

02

Critical infrastructure protection

Energy, borders, logistics and the environment require continuous monitoring and situational awareness.

03

A national aviation industry

Developing domestic aircraft manufacturing builds resilience, expertise and export potential.
